Eminem

Biography

Eminem, born Marshall Bruce Mathers III, is a renowned American rapper, songwriter, and record producer. He gained worldwide fame with his alter ego, Slim Shady, and is known for his powerful lyrics and controversial themes.

Various hit albums created by Eminem include: The Slim Shady LP, The Marshall Mathers LP, The Eminem Show.
